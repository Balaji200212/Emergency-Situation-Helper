# Emergency-Situation-Helper

**Problem Statement**

TNHSP proposed to launch ambulance service which is a 24x7 service, which anyone can avail by dialing the number 108 on their phone during the case of any emergency.
It could be either a medical emergency, a fire emergency, or a crime being committed. The number 108 is a toll-free number and can be dialed from any phone, be it a mobile phone or a landline, at the time of an emergency, without any prefix or suffix.
Help reaches the person within 20 minutes.
Initially, the person who receives the call at Emergency Response Centre (ERC) takes down the nature of emergency and the location of the caller. Depending on the nature of the call, either an ambulance, a fire engine, or a police assistance is sent. This is done through the Emergency Response Centre.
Taking down the location and nature of emergency of the caller through a phone call can take more time. In addition to that the caller might be in panic. He/she may not share the location or the nature of emergency correctly in such situations

**Proposed Solution**

The proposed solution involves two application users, that is, people who need emergency services and emergency service providers.
The part of the application for emergency service providers includes nature of emergency and location conveyance, services filter, emergency booking, settings and about us menu.
On the other hand the part of the application for emergency service providers would include information generation like availability status, alerts and location sharing.
People who need emergency services can choose the nature of emergency and share their location. They can then choose the appropriate service that they need and track the status of the service after booking.
